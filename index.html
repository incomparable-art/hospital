    OPD Services
    "Comprehensive Eye Care, Hassle-Free!"

    Our OPD (Outpatient Department) services provide expert consultations, diagnostic evaluations, and personalized treatment plans for all eye-related concerns. Experience quick, efficient care without long waits.

    Eye Surgery
    "Clear Vision, Brighter Future!"

    Eye surgery is a transformative solution for vision problems such as cataracts, glaucoma, and refractive errors. Modern procedures like LASIK, cataract removal, and corneal transplants offer safe and effective ways to restore clarity and improve quality of life. At Arvind Eye Hospital, our expert ophthalmologists ensure precise, comfortable, and state-of-the-art treatment tailored to your needs.

    Glaucoma Laser Surgery
    "Protect Your Vision from Glaucoma Damage!"

    Glaucoma laser surgery is a precise and effective treatment to lower eye pressure and prevent vision loss. Our advanced laser technology ensures minimal discomfort and maximizes long-term eye health.

    Eye Contouring Treatment
    "Enhance the Beauty of Your Eyes!"

    Eye contouring treatment rejuvenates the delicate skin around your eyes, reducing wrinkles, puffiness, and dark circles. Using advanced techniques, this non-invasive procedure restores youthful, radiant eyes and boosts your confidence.

    Lasik Surgery
    "Say Goodbye to Glasses and Contact Lenses!"

    LASIK surgery is a safe, quick, and effective procedure to correct vision problems like nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism. With advanced laser technology, regain crystal-clear vision and enjoy life without the hassle of eyewear.

    Eye Opretion
    "Advanced Eye Surgery for a Clearer Tomorrow!"

    Eye operations, including cataract removal, LASIK, and retinal procedures, restore vision and improve eye health. Our skilled surgeons use cutting-edge technology to deliver safe, effective results.

    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

    How do I know if I need eye surgery?

    We will recommend surgery based on your condition, symptoms, and test results. Common reasons for surgery include cataracts, vision correction, glaucoma, or retinal issues. Schedule a consultation to learn more.

    Is eye surgery painful?

    Most eye surgeries are not painful. Advanced techniques and anesthesia ensure comfort during the procedure. Post-surgery, you may experience mild discomfort, which typically resolves quickly.

    How long does it take to recover from eye surgery?

    Recovery time varies depending on the procedure. For most surgeries, patients can resume normal activities within a few days to weeks, but full recovery might take longer. Follow your doctor’s instructions for best results.

    How often should I get an eye check-up?

    Every 1-2 years for adults with no symptoms. Annually for those with diabetes, a family history of eye disease, or existing vision problems.

    Can I book an appointment online?

    Yes, you can schedule an appointment through our website or contact our support team via phone. Emergency cases are given priority.

    What precautions should I take after surgery?

    Avoid strenuous activities, keep your eyes protected, follow medication schedules, and attend follow-up appointments. Detailed post-operative care instructions will be provided by your doctor.

    Do you treat children’s eye conditions?

    Yes, we have a dedicated pediatric eye care unit that addresses vision problems, squints, and other eye conditions in children.
